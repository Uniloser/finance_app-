About JIKER Loans

JIKER Loans is a limited liability company established in 2025, specializing in fast, easy, and flexible pocket loans for university and college students. Our core activity is providing secured, affordable credit with immediate electronic funding and flexible repayment terms, designed to fit the unique needs of student life.

JIKER Loans was founded by a group of recent graduates who experienced firsthand the financial challenges students face. United by a shared vision, they set out to create a lending service that was truly for students, by students.
