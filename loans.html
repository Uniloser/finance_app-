Frequently Asked Questions

Is my personal data safe? expand_more

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and robust security protocols to protect your information at all times. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

What happens if I miss a payment? expand_more

We understand that student life can be unpredictable. We offer flexible options, including payment pauses and adjusted schedules. Contact our support team to discuss your situation.

Are there any hidden fees? expand_more

No. JIKER Loans is committed to transparency. We have no origination fees, no prepayment penalties, and no hidden charges. Everything is clearly outlined in your loan agreement.
