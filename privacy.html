Introduction

Welcome to JIKER Loans. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

expand_more

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including personal (name, contact info), financial (bank details, income), and technical data (IP address, device info) to ensure a smooth and secure loan application process.

How We Use Your Information

expand_more

Your information is used for loan processing, identity verification, risk assessment, and communicating with you about your account. We do not use your personal data for purposes other than those disclosed in this policy.

How We Share Your Information

expand_more

We may share your data with trusted partners for identity and credit verification. We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. All sharing is done securely and in compliance with legal standards.

Data Security and Storage

expand_more

We employ state-of-the-art security measures, including end-to-end encryption and strict access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your information is stored on secure servers for the duration required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

expand_more

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your communication preferences at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

Use of Cookies

expand_more

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling them may affect the functionality of our service.

Changes to This Policy

expand_more

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
